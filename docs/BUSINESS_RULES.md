# BUSINESS_RULES.md

## Purpose

The dashboard must support multiple businesses.

It should never assume only one exists.

Businesses should be independent but manageable from one Business HQ.

---

# Business Philosophy

Revenue first.

Systems second.

Automation third.

Perfection last.

The purpose of every business is to create freedom.

---

# Current Business Categories

AI Automation Agency

Digital Products

Amazon FBA

Property

Faceless Content

Future businesses should be easy to add.

---

# Business Metrics

Each business should track:

Revenue

Profit

Monthly Target

Leads

Conversions

Tasks

Current Bottleneck

Priority

Notes

---

# Dashboard Behaviour

Business HQ should answer:

Where should I focus?

What business needs attention?

Which business is making money?

Which business is falling behind?

---

# Daily Priority

Each business should display:

Current Task

Next Action

Estimated Impact

Priority Level

Progress

---

# Long-Term Goal

The dashboard should eventually manage unlimited businesses from one location.
